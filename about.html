About Excel-IQ

Excel-IQ is a platform designed to help professionals understand, analyze, and act on their business data using Excel and AI.

Why Excel-IQ Exists

Many businesses rely heavily on spreadsheets, but turning data into insight is often difficult. Reports become complex, dashboards take time to build, and valuable information remains hidden in raw data.

Excel-IQ was created to simplify this process by combining practical Excel learning, ready-to-use dashboards, and AI-powered analytics.

Our goal is simple: help professionals transform data into decisions.
